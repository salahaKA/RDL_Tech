Powerful IoT Edge Capabilities Intelligent Data Logger

Intelligent Data Logger product is designed to seamlessly integrate with the IoT and Analytical processing systems. Supporting multiple I/O options, interfaces data logger is a perfect fit to build custom automation solutions.



Highly Reliable Connectivity

Dual SIM & Auto link carrier detection and network switching features ensure uninterrupted internet access . Embedded watchdog keeps the device always online.




No Coding - Plug & Play

The Plug & Play device feature eliminates the need for coding and allows you to quickly connect your device to the most popular IoT public and private platforms.




Supporting Wide Range of Industrial Protocol

Device offers multiple industrial protocols like MODBUS RTU , MODBUS TCP , JSON , MQTT , FTP and support secure communication SSL.




Industrial Isolated IO

Intelligent Data logger offers multiple isolated IO to monitor PLC and, VFD , Motors, servo, Valves, energy meter, actuators, relays, encoder, RFID and finger print readers, industrial sensors




Alarm & Automated Task

Device Send emergency alerts and different escalation level SMS to the remote users on a real time basis . Device also supports automating the remote asset based on a set threshold




Easy Remote Management

You can easily monitor onsite devices health and bulk configuration through remote access or remote maintenance solution .




Industrial Grade Design

Product designed to withstand with industrial noise and Harsh Environmental Conditions Isolated & Protected IO & EMC / EMI compliance .
